About FetchEstate

FetchEstate is a property marketplace designed for users in Pakistan. The app helps individuals, property owners, and agents to buy, sell, and rent residential and commercial properties with ease and confidence.

Key Features

  • Post and manage property listings
  • Search properties by location and category
  • Chat directly with buyers and sellers
  • Save properties to your wishlist
  • Verified and user-managed listings

Privacy & User Control

We respect your privacy and provide full control over your data. Users can update or delete their accounts directly from within the app.
