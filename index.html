About FetchEstate

FetchEstate is a mobile-based property marketplace designed for users in Pakistan. It connects property owners, agents, and buyers, allowing them to post listings, search properties, and communicate directly through the app.

Key Features

  • Create and manage property listings
  • Search properties by location, price, and category
  • In-app chat between buyers and sellers
  • Save properties to wishlist
  • User-managed listings (Owner / Agent)
  • Secure authentication using Firebase

Privacy, Legal & User Control

We respect user privacy and provide transparency regarding data usage. Users can manage their profiles, control permissions, and delete their accounts directly from within the app.

Privacy Policy Terms & Conditions

Account Deletion

FetchEstate allows users to permanently delete their account from within the app. Deleting an account removes profile data, listings, and authentication records.
